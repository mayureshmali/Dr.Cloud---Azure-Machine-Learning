OUR GOAL

Improve cardiovascular health and quality of life through prevention, detection, and treatment of risk factors for heart attack and stroke; early identification and treatment of heart attacks and strokes; prevention of repeat cardiovascular events; and reduction in deaths from cardiovascular disease

Objective One

Increase overall cardiovascular health in the U.S. population

Objective Two

Reduce coronary heart disease and stroke deaths

Objective Three

Create Awareness amongst people about stroke

Objective Four

Help doctors to take proactive measures

Objective Five

Reduce hospitalization of older adults

Objective Six

Improve Patient-Doctor engagement
